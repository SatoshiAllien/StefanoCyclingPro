FIRMA CODICE — OBBLIGATORIO per iPhone + Apple Watch
=====================================================

1. Apri Config/Development.xcconfig
2. Inserisci il tuo Team ID Apple:
   DEVELOPMENT_TEAM = AB12CD34EF

   (Trovalo su https://developer.apple.com/account → Membership)

3. In Xcode: Product → Clean Build Folder (⇧⌘K)
4. Build (⌘B)

Oppure in Xcode:
- Target StefanoCyclingPro → Signing & Capabilities → Team
- Target StefanoCyclingProWatch → Signing & Capabilities → Team

Senza Team ID vedrai: "requires a development team"
